# Idea Spark

**Idea Spark** is an AI-powered **Startup Idea Validator**. Enter a startup idea or product concept, and get instant insights, suggestions, and outputs that help founders validate and improve their ideas.

## Features

- **AI-Generated Taglines**  
  Quickly get catchy and relevant taglines for your idea.

- **Pitch Deck & One-Pager Suggestions**  
  Generate sections for a mini pitch deck or investor-ready one-pager.

- **Target Audience Insights**  
  AI suggests detailed buyer personas, demographics, and pain points.

- **Simulated Customer Interviews**  
  Role-play potential customers asking questions and providing feedback.

- **Idea Improvement Suggestions**  
  Get AI recommendations to refine, differentiate, and scale your idea.

- **Idea Validation Scoring**  
  Evaluate ideas based on market size, competition, uniqueness, and traction potential.

- **Spin-Off Ideas**  
  Generate related ideas or variations to explore new opportunities.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn-ui
- **Build Tool:** Vite
- **AI:** Lovable
- **Backend**: Node js, deployed on Cloud
